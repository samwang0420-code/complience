## Our Comprehensive Roof Repair Services in Oakland

We provide a full spectrum of repair services to address every issue your Oakland roof may encounter.

### **Wind & Storm Damage Repair**
Oakland’s powerful gusts, especially during seasonal transitions, can tear off shingles, loosen flashing, and damage roof edges. We repair and secure wind-lifted shingles, replace missing ones with exact matches, and reinforce eaves and rakes to prevent future uplift.

### **Hail Damage Assessment & Repair**
Hailstorms, while occasional, can cause significant bruising or cracking to asphalt shingles and wood shakes, compromising their lifespan. We conduct meticulous inspections to identify hail damage often missed by the untrained eye and perform precise shingle or shake replacement to restore roof integrity.

### **Leak Repair & Water Intrusion**
Wind-driven rain exploits the smallest weakness. We specialize in tracing elusive leaks to their source, which is often far from the interior water stain. Repairs focus on critical leak-prone areas: around chimneys, skylights, vent pipes, and in valleys where roof planes meet.

### **Moss & Algae Remediation**
The moist, shady conditions in many Oakland neighborhoods are a breeding ground for moss, which lifts shingles and retains moisture, leading to rot. We perform safe, effective moss removal and apply treatments to inhibit regrowth, often coupled with repairs to any underlying wood damage.

### **Wood Rot & Decking Repair**
Common in older wood shake roofs, rot can weaken the roof deck (sheathing). We remove damaged shakes, replace rotted decking sections, and install new underlayment and materials to create a solid, stable base.

### **Flashing & Sealant Failure**
Failed flashing—the metal strips around protrusions and valleys—is a leading cause of leaks. We repair or replace corroded, bent, or poorly sealed flashing with durable, code-compliant materials, using proper techniques for Oakland’s driving rains.

### **Emergency Tarping & Temporary Repairs**
For sudden, severe damage, our 24/7 emergency service secures your property with professional-grade tarping to prevent interior water damage until permanent repairs can be scheduled.

### **Roof Maintenance & Inspections**
Prevention is key. Our maintenance programs include gutter clearing, debris removal, and bi-annual inspections to catch and address minor issues—like a cracked shingle or clogged valley—before they become major, costly repairs.
