## The Challenge: Invisible Despite Market Dominance

Miami Implant Specialists was paradoxically both **market-dominant and AI-invisible**. They held the #1 Google ranking for "dental implants Miami" yet were hemorrhaging high-value patients to competitors through AI search diversion.

### The Discovery

Dr. Marcus Rodriguez, the practice owner and lead prosthodontist, identified the problem during a quarterly competitive review:

> "I was reviewing our competitor across town—they opened 3 years after us and have fewer credentials. Yet when I asked Perplexity 'Who's the best dental implant surgeon in Miami?' they were #1. We're #1 on Google, but we're invisible where it matters most for high-intent patients."

### Why AI Invisibility Was Particularly Devastating

Dental implant patients exhibit specific high-value behaviors:
- **Long research cycle:** 3-6 months from initial research to procedure
- **High consideration:** Average patient consults 2.4 practices
- **Complex decision:** Requires trust in surgical expertise
- **Premium pricing:** Full-mouth cases range $20K-$60K
- **Lifetime value:** Single implant patients often return for additional work

When these high-intent patients ask AI for recommendations, the AI's answer becomes their shortlist. Being excluded means complete market invisibility to the most valuable patient segment.
