# ChatGPT vs. Perplexity vs. Claude: Which AI Engine Sends You the Most Patients?

## The Fragmented AI Landscape

Not all AI engines work the same way. And not all AI engines matter equally for your medical practice.

Understanding the differences between ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google SGE is critical for prioritizing your GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) investment.

## Platform-Specific Patient Behavior

### ChatGPT (OpenAI)

**Patient Profile:**
- Tech-savvy, early adopters
- Ages 25-45
- Higher education levels
- Comfortable with AI recommendations

**Query Patterns:**
> "What's the best Botox clinic near me?"
> "Should I get implants or veneers?"
> "How much does a facelift cost in Beverly Hills?"

**Recommendation Style:**
- Provides 2-4 specific practice names
- Includes brief reasoning
- Often cites "according to reviews" or "highly rated"
- Less likely to provide detailed citations

**Medical Practice Impact:**
- **High-value aesthetic procedures** (Botox, fillers, laser)
- **Elective surgeries** (augmentation, rhinoplasty)
- **Wellness treatments** (IV therapy, hormone optimization)

**Visibility Requirements:**
- Strong review presence (Google, RealSelf)
- Local authority signals
- Clear service differentiation

### Perplexity AI

**Patient Profile:**
- Research-oriented decision makers
- Ages 30-55
- Higher income, analytical mindset
- Want citations and evidence

**Query Patterns:**
> "Compare the top 5 dental implant surgeons in Miami"
> "What are the risks of [procedure] and which clinics have the best safety records?"
> "Evidence-based analysis of [treatment] effectiveness"

**Recommendation Style:**
- Provides detailed, cited responses
- Links to sources
- Comparative analysis
- Emphasizes factual accuracy

**Medical Practice Impact:**
- **High-stakes procedures** (implants, surgery)
- **Research-intensive decisions** (fertility, oncology)
- **Expensive treatments** (full mouth reconstruction, multiple procedures)

**Visibility Requirements:**
- Citations in authoritative sources
- Published research or case studies
- Detailed service documentation
- Professional credentials prominently featured

### Claude (Anthropic)

**Patient Profile:**
- Quality-focused
- Ages 28-50
- Values nuanced, thoughtful responses
- Often asking complex questions

**Query Patterns:**
> "I'm considering a mommy makeover. What should I know?"
> "Help me understand the difference between these three approaches to [procedure]"
> "What questions should I ask during a consultation for [treatment]?"

**Recommendation Style:**
- Balanced, nuanced recommendations
- Explains trade-offs
- Often provides educational context
- May recommend based on specific patient needs

**Medical Practice Impact:**
- **Complex decision scenarios** (multiple procedures)
- **First-time patients** (education-heavy)
- **Anxiety-driven queries** ("Is this safe?")

**Visibility Requirements:**
- Educational content depth
- Patient education resources
- Consultation-focused approach
- Empathy and patient care emphasis

### Google Search Generative Experience (SGE)

**Patient Profile:**
- Broad demographic
- Traditional Google users
- May not realize they're using AI
- High trust in Google brand

**Query Patterns:**
> "best plastic surgeon los angeles"
> "dental implants cost near me"
> "med spa reviews"

**Recommendation Style:**
- Integrated with traditional search
- AI-generated overview at top
- Still shows traditional results below
- Emphasizes local and verified information

**Medical Practice Impact:**
- **Local searches** ("near me")
- **Cost-conscious patients** (price queries)
- **Comparison shopping** ("best" + location)

**Visibility Requirements:**
- Google Business Profile optimization
- Local SEO strength
- Review velocity and recency
- Traditional SEO still matters

## Platform-Specific GEO Strategies

### For ChatGPT Optimization

**Priority Tactics:**
1. **Review Volume Strategy**
   - Target: 200+ reviews per location
   - Focus on: Google, RealSelf, Yelp
   - Response rate: 90%+

2. **Local Entity Building**
   - Consistent NAP across 50+ directories
   - Location-specific content clusters
   - Neighborhood authority signals

3. **Service Clarity**
   - Clear procedure descriptions
   - Before/after galleries
   - Pricing transparency (ranges)

**Timeline to Visibility:** 60-90 days

### For Perplexity Optimization

**Priority Tactics:**
1. **Authority Citation Building**
   - Medical forum presence (Reddit, RealSelf)
   - Guest publications (Dentistry Today, etc.)
   - Research citations

2. **Detailed Documentation**
   - Procedure deep-dives
   - Outcome statistics
   - Comparative analysis content

3. **Credential Visibility**
   - Board certification verification
   - Professional association membership
   - Speaking/publishing history

**Timeline to Visibility:** 90-120 days

### For Claude Optimization

**Priority Tactics:**
1. **Educational Content Depth**
   - Patient decision guides
   - FAQ expansiveness
   - Risk/benefit analysis

2. **Empathy Signals**
   - Patient story documentation
   - Anxiety-addressing content
   - Consultation preparation guides

3. **Consultation Focus**
   - Booking ease emphasis
   - Consultation value proposition
   - Patient journey documentation

**Timeline to Visibility:** 75-105 days

### For Google SGE Optimization

**Priority Tactics:**
1. **Local SEO Foundation**
   - Google Business Profile optimization
   - Local citation consistency
   - Local content clusters

2. **Review Acceleration**
   - Review generation programs
   - Response management
   - Photo/video reviews

3. **Structured Data**
   - Medical schema markup
   - FAQ schema
   - Local business schema

**Timeline to Visibility:** 45-75 days

## The Bottom Line

Different AI engines send different types of patients. The key is matching your platform strategy to:

1. **Your specialty** (aesthetics vs. surgery vs. dentistry)
2. **Your patient demographics** (age, tech-savviness, research behavior)
3. **Your procedure values** (quick treatments vs. complex surgeries)
4. **Your competitive landscape** (where competitors are visible)

**The medical practices winning in the AI era aren't optimizing for all platforms equally.**

They're prioritizing the platforms that send their ideal patients—and dominating those channels.
