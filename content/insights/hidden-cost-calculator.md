# The Hidden Cost of AI Invisibility: A Revenue Calculator for Medical Practices

## The Patient Journey Has Changed

Five years ago, the typical patient journey looked like this:

1. **Awareness** – "I'm interested in breast augmentation"
2. **Research** – Google search → browse 5-10 websites
3. **Consideration** – Read reviews, compare options
4. **Decision** – Book consultation with top 2-3 choices

**Today, that same journey looks radically different:**

1. **Awareness** – "I'm interested in breast augmentation"
2. **AI Query** – "What's the best breast augmentation surgeon in [city]?"
3. **AI Recommendation** – Patient receives 2-3 specific practice recommendations
4. **Decision** – Books consultation directly

**The research and consideration phases have collapsed into a single AI recommendation.**
